Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
    2
  • Ripe avocado
    1
  • Salt
    To taste
  • Black pepper
    To taste
  • Lemon juice
    1 tsp
  • Olive oil (optional)
    1 tsp
  • Red pepper flakes
    A pinch

Steps

Step 1
Toast the bread slices until crisp and golden.
00:30
Step 2
Cut the avocado, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
00:40
Step 3
Mash the avocado with a fork, keeping some small chunks.
00:45
Step 4
Season with salt, black pepper, and lemon juice.
00:30
Step 5
Spread the mixture onto the toasted bread slices.
00:25
Step 6
Top with red pepper flakes or olive oil. Serve immediately.
00:20
